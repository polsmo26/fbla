Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

Account & Technical Support

How do I create an account on Workly? Joining Workly is quick and straightforward. Simply click the "Get In!" button located at the top of the page, and you'll be guided through the registration process. You'll need to fill in basic information such as your name and username, select if you an applicant or employer, and finally create a secure password. Once your account is created, you can start browsing available job listings, saving your favorite opportunities, and applying directly through the platform. Building out your profile with skills, work experience, and a resume will help employers find you more easily.

Can I edit my profile after creating it? Yes, your Workly profile is fully customizable and can be updated whenever you like. Once you log into your account, navigate to your profile page and select the "Edit Profile" option. From there, you can update your bio, add new skills, upload a fresh resume, and modify your work experience to keep your profile current. Keeping your information up to date increases your chances of being noticed by employers and ensures you’re matched with jobs that fit your qualifications.

Website Issues

Why am I having trouble logging in? If you’re having difficulty logging into your account, start by double-checking that you're not using a common password and make sure your password matches! Pay attention to any capitalization or extra spaces, as passwords are case-sensitive. Occasionally, high traffic may cause brief login issues, so waiting a few minutes and trying again can also resolve the problem.

Why isn't my job application going through? There are a few common reasons why an application might not submit properly. First, double-check that all required fields are completed accurately, including your resume upload, cover letter, and any additional questions the employer has provided. Ensure your resume is in an accepted format (such as PDF or DOCX) and doesn’t exceed the file size limit. If you continue to experience issues, try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or switching to a different browser. If problems persist, please reach out to our support team for assistance.

What should I do if a job listing seems outdated or incorrect? We strive to keep our job listings as accurate and up-to-date as possible. However, if you come across a job posting that appears to be outdated, closed, or contains incorrect information, we encourage you to let us know. You can report the listing directly by clicking the "Contact" button found on the navigation bar. Provide any relevant details so our team can review and take appropriate action. Your feedback helps us maintain a high-quality experience for all users and ensures that the opportunities on Workly remain reliable.
